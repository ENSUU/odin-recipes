Shrimp Marinara

Description:

Shrimp marinara hits all the right spots - tasty shrimp with homemade tomato sauce served over spaghetti. A quick and easy weeknight meal.

Ingredients:

  • Pasta
  • Oil
  • Shrimp
  • Seasoning
  • Canned Tomatoes
  • Balsamic Vinegar

Steps:

  1. Make the spaghetti.
  2. Cook the shrimp and transfer to a plate.
  3. Cook the garlic and red pepper to the skillet.
  4. Stir the remaining ingredients and bing to a boil. Then, simmer until thickened
  5. Return the shrimp to the skillet and cook until heated.
  6. Serve over spaghetti and garnish with fresh basil.
